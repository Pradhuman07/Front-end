Relative==> Children div agr absolute hoga to ooper uthega aur khi bhi ghum skega..parent div ke bahr bhi (kyuki absolute mtlb as reference to screen)
    ...If we want ki wo parent ke andr hi rhe aur jo properties hm lga rhe h like top se 50% to wo as reference with screen na ho as reference to parent div ho(that means hm use screen ke center me nhi lana chahte parent div ke center me lana chahte h) to parent div ko "position:relative" dedo aur child div ko position absolute(absolute dena hi pdega tbhi to uthega na wo ooper..aur parent ko relative bnadia jisse wo usse child ko control me rkh rhe h aur use bahr nhi ghumne de rhe)

Transform==> Distance hmesha top left corner se hota h isiliye to tum sirf top = 50%,left:50% dete ho to div box ka top left corner centre me aata h na ki tumhara div box.... 
                iska soln is "transform: translate(-50%,-50%)"....isiliye lgate h isko..ye
jo iski position hoti h us position se half side left ko aur half side ooper ko shift krdeta h   
        (-50%,-50%) :- mtlb -50% x-axis pr aur -50% y axis pr khisak jao aadha portion 

GPT:-

1.Child div ko parent div ke center me laane ke liye, parent div ko position: relative dena hoga, aur child div ko position: absolute dena padega.
2.Iske saath saath, top: 50%, left: 50% use karoge,
3.lekin isse child div exactly center me nahi aayega kyunki top aur left ke 50% values child div ke top-left corner ko parent div ke center par le aayengi.
4.Is problem ko solve karne ke liye, child div ka transform: translate(-50%, -50%) bhi add karna hoga, jisse child div ka center parent div ke center par align ho jayega

#parent {
  width: 500px;
  height: 500px;
  background-color: lightgray;
  position: relative;
}

#child {
  width: 100px;
  height: 100px;
  background-color: red;
  position: absolute;
  top: 50%;
  left: 50%;
  transform: translate(-50%, -50%);
}

Doubt:-but sirf child div ko relative krdo aur parent div ko kuch position na do to bhi to kam horha h agr hm baki cheezon ko as it is rk the

Ans:-Haan, agar aap sirf position: relative child div ko de rahe ho, aur parent div ko koi position nahi de rahe, aur baaki CSS properties (like top: 50%, left: 50%, and transform: translate(-50%, -50%)) as it is rakhte ho, tab bhi child div visually center me dikhai dega.

Lekin yah cheez technical perspective se correct approach nahi ha.
Is case me child div parent div ke andar visually center ho sakta hai, par ye zyada reliable nahi hota hai complex layouts ya nested divs ke case me.

Best practice: Parent ko position: relative do aur child ko position: absolute. Ye approach zyada reliable aur scalable hota hai.
